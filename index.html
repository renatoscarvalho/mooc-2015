Teaching with technology and inquiry

An open course for teachers

Watch the video
Enroll now

Starts on July 1, 2015, at edX

a free course designed with k-12 teachers in mind.

This is a MOOC for teachers in STEAM topics (science, technology engineering, arts, math) that want to learn about adding inquiry and technology lessons to their classrooms. In this course you will find a large community of teachers from around the world with whom you will be able to share resources and exchange ideas about what works for specific topics and age groups and also engage in design groups to collaboratively develop a new lesson.

Including a design strand and get even more from the course.

Foundations Strand

Complete INQ101x and obtain a certificate through the foundations strand and engage in reflection, peer discussion, and lesson design evaluation.

Design Strand

Add another experience level to your course. Become deeply involved in lesson design with your peers by entering the Design strand.

Research and practice together to provide a BROAD overview of teaching with technology and inquiry.

Instructors are from the two worlds of research and practice - a professor and a school principal who team up to share their experiences and lead you to reflect on examples and even design new lessons of your own. In addition, each week a different UTS teacher will present real examples of teaching with technology and inquiry directly from their classes.
